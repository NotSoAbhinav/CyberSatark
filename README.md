CyberSatark is a phishing awareness website built by the WiCYS Club to educate users about phishing and social-engineering attacks. Many users fall victim to scams due to lack of awareness; CyberSatark addresses this by providing clear examples of legitimate vs fraudulent communications, practical identification guidelines, and interactive learning modules.

The platform allows users to explore real-world phishing scenarios, analyze suspicious messages using a checklist-based approach, and test their knowledge through quizzes. By simplifying cybersecurity concepts and focusing on everyday digital interactions such as emails, SMS, and social media alerts, CyberSatark promotes safer online behavior and proactive threat recognition.

Key Features

Real vs fake email and message examples

Phishing identification checklist

Interactive awareness quiz

Educational content on phishing & social engineering

Designed for students and general users
